`Whats new

  • DENNS CAFE

    The School shows concern for children’s’ nutrition. Students are encouraged to bring wholesome and nutritious food from home. Chips, pizzas, burgers or any other junk food items are not encouraged on the campus. Breakfast/lunch at school will be supervised by teachers to encourage good table etiquette.

  • Eduflex

    At Deens Academy, we believe that parental engagement can positively influence pupil learning and progress. Eduflex is a School Information Management Software (ERP) created by Flexiapps Solutions Pvt Ltd that enables important information from the School to reach you. This software powers our admission application form and enables parents to create and update student information directly. Later, Alerts, notices, circulars, school calendar and newsletters will be sent to parents and students through Eduflex on a regular basis. Assessment reports too will be sent to individual students on their respective ids. Parents can also update personal details such as photographs, phone numbers, etc. Fees receipts can be downloaded from Eduflex directly. Parents are requested to check ‘Eduflex parent portal’ daily for updates.
